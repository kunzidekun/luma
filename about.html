Who We Are

PrimeCare Medical is a professional medical equipment supplier based in Guangzhou, China. With over 15 years of experience, we have been providing high-quality medical devices and hospital solutions to healthcare facilities across more than 60 countries worldwide.

We partner with leading manufacturers and maintain strict quality control processes to ensure every product meets international standards including ISO 13485, CE, and FDA requirements. Our product range covers clinical analysis, surgical equipment, diagnostic imaging, hospital furniture, and medical consumables.
